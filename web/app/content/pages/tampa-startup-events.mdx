## Startup events in Tampa Bay

The Tampa Bay area hosts a variety of startup-oriented events:

- **Pitch nights and demo days** - opportunities to present ideas or watch startups pitch to investors and the community
- **Founder meetups** - networking events specifically for startup founders and co-founders
- **Startup networking** - community mixers bringing together founders, investors, operators, and advisors
- **Accelerator and incubator events** - info sessions, workshops, and open houses from local programs
- **Entrepreneurship workshops** - practical sessions on fundraising, sales, product, hiring, and other startup topics
- **Investor meetups** - events connecting founders with angel investors and VCs active in Tampa Bay
