Tampa Bay is home to a broad range of developer meetups covering programming languages, frameworks, cloud platforms, and more. Tampa.dev aggregates these events into a single calendar so you can find what's happening without checking a dozen different sites.

## Types of developer meetups in Tampa Bay

The Tampa Bay area hosts meetups across the full spectrum of software development:

- **Languages & frameworks** - JavaScript/TypeScript, Python, Rust, Go, Java, .NET, Ruby, PHP, React, Angular, Vue, and more
- **Cloud & infrastructure** - AWS, Azure, GCP user groups, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, and DevOps communities
- **Data & AI** - Data engineering, machine learning, data science, and AI study groups
- **Security** - Cybersecurity meetups, CTF events, OWASP chapter meetings
- **Mobile & web** - iOS, Android, React Native, Flutter, and web development groups
- **General software engineering** - Code & Coffee, agile/lean meetups, architecture discussions

## Finding the right meetup

Use the [Tampa Bay tech events calendar](/calendar) to browse upcoming meetups. You can filter by group to focus on communities that match your interests, or use the [event map](/map) to find meetups near your location.

Every group listed on Tampa.dev has its own [group page](/groups) with a description, upcoming events, and links to their community channels.

## Getting involved

Most developer meetups in Tampa Bay are free and open to anyone. Many welcome talks and lightning talks from attendees. Check individual group pages for details on how to get involved, give a talk, or suggest a topic.
