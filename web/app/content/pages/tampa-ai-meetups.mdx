## AI and ML events in Tampa Bay

The region hosts a range of AI-focused gatherings:

- **AI/ML meetups** - regular meetup groups focused on machine learning, deep learning, and applied AI
- **Data science communities** - groups covering data engineering, analytics, and statistical modeling
- **Study groups** - collaborative learning sessions for AI papers, courses, and new technologies
- **Tech talks** - presentations on LLMs, NLP, computer vision, MLOps, and emerging AI tools
- **Cross-disciplinary events** - AI topics at broader developer meetups and startup events
