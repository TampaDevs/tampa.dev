Tampa Bay has a large and active tech community. Tampa.dev is a neutral, community-run calendar that aggregates tech events from independent groups across the region - including Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Brandon, Sarasota, and surrounding areas.

## What kinds of events are listed?

Tampa.dev tracks a wide range of tech events across the Tampa Bay area:

- **Developer meetups** - language-specific groups (JavaScript, Python, Rust, Go), framework communities (React, .NET, Rails), and general software engineering
- **Startup and founder events** - pitch nights, demo days, founder meetups, and startup networking
- **AI and data science** - machine learning study groups, AI meetups, and data engineering talks
- **Cloud and infrastructure** - AWS, Azure, GCP user groups, DevOps meetups, and Kubernetes communities
- **Cybersecurity** - security meetups, CTF events, and infosec talks
- **Design and product** - UX/UI meetups, product management groups
- **Hackathons and workshops** - hands-on coding events and build days

## How it works

Tampa.dev automatically syncs events from dozens of community groups. Events are listed with date, time, venue, and RSVP links. You can filter by group, event type (in-person or online), and subscribe to a calendar feed to get updates in your preferred calendar app.
