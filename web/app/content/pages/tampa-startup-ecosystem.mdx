Tampa Bay has a growing startup ecosystem that spans Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, and the surrounding region. Tampa.dev serves as a neutral events calendar for the community, aggregating events from independent groups, accelerators, coworking spaces, and meetup organizers.

## What makes up Tampa Bay's startup ecosystem

The Tampa Bay startup community includes:

- **Meetup groups and communities** - regular gatherings for founders, developers, and builders
- **Coworking spaces** - shared workspaces that host events and build community
- **Accelerators and incubators** - programs that support early-stage startups
- **Angel and investor networks** - groups connecting founders with capital
- **University programs** - entrepreneurship programs at USF, UT, and other institutions
- **Industry groups** - communities focused on specific verticals like healthtech, fintech, and edtech

## Building in Tampa Bay

Tampa Bay offers practical advantages for building a company:

- **Eastern time zone** - aligned with New York, Washington DC, and Boston for East Coast business hours
- **Cost of living** - lower than many comparable East Coast metro areas
- **Talent pool** - a growing number of software engineers, designers, and operators in the region
- **Community** - an active, accessible network of founders and builders

Tampa.dev helps you stay connected to this community through a shared events calendar and [open APIs for builders](/builders).
