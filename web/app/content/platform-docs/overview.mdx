# Tampa.dev Overview

Tampa.dev is the community platform for Tampa Bay's tech community. It brings together local tech groups, events, and people into a single place where you can discover what's happening, connect with other developers, and track your involvement.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** — Create an account to unlock your profile, favorites, and badges.
2. **Explore groups** — Browse the [Groups directory](/groups) to find meetups, communities, and organizations that match your interests.
3. **Attend events** — Check the [Events page](/events) or the [Calendar](/calendar) for upcoming meetups, workshops, and socials across the Tampa Bay area.
4. **Earn badges** — Participate in events and community activities to earn badges and climb the leaderboard.
5. **Build your portfolio** — Add portfolio items to your profile to showcase your work to the community.

## Key Features

### Events Aggregation

Tampa.dev automatically syncs events from platforms like Meetup, Eventbrite, and Luma so you can see everything happening in one place. You can also view events on a [map](/map) to find meetups near you.

### Groups

Groups represent local tech communities, meetups, and organizations. Each group has its own page with upcoming events, member lists, and a group leaderboard. You can favorite groups to keep track of the ones you care about most.

### Badges & XP

Earn badges by attending events, checking in, and participating in community activities. Each badge awards XP points, and your total XP determines your rank on the [leaderboard](/leaderboard). Badges come in different rarity tiers from Common to Legendary.

### Leaderboards

See how you stack up against other community members on the platform-wide leaderboard, or check out group-specific leaderboards to see who's most active in a particular community.

### Your Profile

Your [profile](/profile) is your home on Tampa.dev. Add a bio, social links, hero image, and theme color to make it your own. Other community members can view your public profile to see your badges, groups, and portfolio items.
